# HireFlow AI

> Crack interviews. Track jobs. Improve with AI.

HireFlow AI is an AI-powered career platform that helps candidates prepare for interviews, manage job applications, and improve performance with intelligent feedback.

---

## Features

###  AI Mock Interviews

* Practice real interview questions
* Simulate real interview scenarios
* Get instant AI-driven feedback

### Job Application Tracker

* Track all applications in one dashboard
* Monitor interview stages and deadlines
* Stay organized throughout your job search

###  AI Feedback System

* Analyze your responses
* Identify weak areas
* Improve communication and problem-solving

###  Performance Analytics

* Track your progress over time
* Visualize strengths and weaknesses
* Optimize your preparation strategy

---

##  Tech Stack

* **Frontend:** Next.js, React, Tailwind CSS
* **Animations:** Framer Motion, Lottie
* **Deployment:** Vercel
